about us

Our Story

Bo started his business in the end of 2007.

The company of Shaoxing Xiao Bo Curtain Products Co., Ltd was established in Jan 2008.

The first fully automated blinds fabric cutting machine was developed and manufactured by us during 2013-2014. which can cut a piece of one roller blind fabric in average 12 seconds.

We signed the exclusive agency agreement about the drill free installation system with German company of tesa nie wieder bohren GmbH (Tesa NWB) for big China region in 2015 which makes us the first company to apply NWB’s technology onto roller blinds installation.

We successfully developed a end-cap lock equipment for the packing box of roller blinds in 2016,which can improve 6-8 times of efficiency compare with hand work. Also made the 4 sides end-cap stapler machine in same year.

Another company with the name of Shaoxing Dou Yang Curtains Co., Ltd was found in Sep 2017.

The technology of Radi-cool was applied on roller blinds in 2017-2018.

Our Australian company of H-Bo Pty Ltd was formed in Apr 2018.

We invented and patented width adjustable blinds in 2019.

Auguest 2020 we started custom made business in Australia.
